Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a traditional Brunei wedding cost?

A traditional Brunei wedding typically costs between BND 30,000-60,000 (USD 22,000-44,000), including all ceremonies and celebrations.

How long do Brunei wedding celebrations last?

Traditional Brunei weddings last 7-10 days, though urban celebrations may be condensed to 3-5 days while maintaining core customs.

What is the Bersanding ceremony?

Bersanding is the grand reception where newlyweds sit on an ornate throne (pelamin), receiving blessings from 200-500 guests.

What happens during Merisik?

Merisik is a traditional inquiry ceremony where family elders assess the bride's availability and compatibility under Islamic principles.

What is Berbedak Mandi?

Berbedak Mandi is a pre-wedding blessing ritual where family members apply colored rice powder and fragrant oils to the couple.

How many guests attend Brunei weddings?

Typical Brunei weddings host 100-300 guests for main ceremonies, while rural celebrations can involve 500-1,000 participants.

What is Menghantar Berian?

Menghantar Berian is an elaborate gift delivery ceremony where the groom's family presents 7-9 decorated hantaran trays.

What happens during Akad Nikah?

Akad Nikah is the Islamic marriage contract ceremony conducted by a kadi (religious officiant) with the bride's wali present.

What is Mulih 3 Hari?

Mulih 3 Hari is a post-wedding tradition where the groom visits the bride's family home three days after the wedding.

How has COVID-19 affected Brunei weddings?

70% of ceremonies now incorporate livestreaming and virtual participation options, adapting traditions to modern safety needs.
