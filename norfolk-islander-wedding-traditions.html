What is Kava Shearin?

Kava Shearin is a bonding ritual where 10-30 family members share traditional kava root drink, connecting to Pitcairn maritime heritage.

Can non-residents marry on Norfolk Island?

Yes, non-residents can marry on Norfolk Island but must file a Notice of Intended Marriage at least one month before.

What happens at the Piknik Brunch?

The Piknik Brunch is a casual 2-hour post-wedding gathering featuring local seafood, informal storytelling, and community celebration.
